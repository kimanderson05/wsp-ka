OUR TEAM

Annette Celender

Annette Celender, LVT graduated from the Median School of Allied Health, Veterinary Technology program and has been with St. Francis since 1999. She has been in the veterinary field for over 29 years and has a special interest in cats that are considered "un-adoptable."

Dr. William Cooper

Dr. William Cooper received his DVM degree from Auburn University in 1984 and worked in both Florida and Chicago, IL before returning to his home town to open St. Francis Hospital for Animals in 1996.
An animal lover since childhood, Dr. Cooper now lives with his wife, Carol, their six cats, two dogs, and two horses.

Ashley Mauldin

Our head technician, Ashley Mauldin, has been with St. Francis since 1996 and was instrumental in getting the practice running. A "jack-of-all-trades", Ashley works every department from reception to surgery.

Stop by to meet the rest of our wonderful staff. You'll see what makes us the best in town!
